What is this document?

This is the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) file for Slashdot. As its name implies, it is intended to address questions that the Slashdot crew are asked repeatedly.

This particular edition is version 1.2.

Updated by: Robo
Last Modified: 12/17/01

I have a question that is not answered in this FAQ. What should I do?

Email your questions to CmdrTaco, Samzenpus, or Robo. We'll try our best to get you an answer, and if it's a question we've seen before, we'll consider adding it to the FAQ.

Updated by: Robo
Last Modified: 12/17/01

Who is responsible for the FAQ?

The FAQ was originally composed by Rob "CmdrTaco" Malda, and indeed much of the verbiage in it is his. In June of 2000, Joe Merlino was hired by Andover.net to revamp it. Joe updated, edited, and reformatted it, and unified it with a number of other documents. The FAQ went through another general update at the end of 2001 by Samzenpus and Robo.
